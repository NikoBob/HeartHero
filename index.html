      Simple by Design

      How HeartHero Works

      A four-step loop that turns a minute of reflection into a score, a trend, and a personalised insight.

      01 ✏️

      Daily Check-In

      Enter your resting heart rate, sleep hours, stress level, exercise minutes, and any symptoms you noticed today.

      02 ✅

      Input Validation

      HeartHero checks that every value is realistic and within a safe range before running the score calculation.

      03 📊

      Score Calculation

      A rule-based algorithm starts at 100 and deducts points for concerning patterns — elevated heart rate, low sleep, high stress, and symptoms.

      04 📋

      Track & Reflect

      Each check-in is saved to your browser's local storage and shown as a history card, a trend bar, and a personalised insight message.

      The Story Behind It

      Why I Built HeartHero

      HeartHero was inspired by my dad's experience with a heart attack, which made me think more seriously about how people track everyday health signals before they become larger concerns. I became interested in how simple factors like stress, sleep, activity, heart rate, and symptoms could be organised in one place to help users better understand their own patterns.

      "By turning a short daily check-in into a score, history, trends, and simple insights, the app makes health feel less abstract and more visible."

      I built HeartHero to explore how technology can make those everyday signals easier to understand. The goal is not to diagnose anyone or replace a doctor. Instead, HeartHero is meant to help people slow down, check in with themselves, and notice patterns that might otherwise be ignored.

      For me, this project is not just about building a web app. It is about using code to respond to a real problem I saw in my own life. HeartHero reflects my interest in preventative health, human-centered design, and building tools that make complicated information easier for people to act on.

      Your Data Stays Yours

      Privacy & Safety

      🔒
      Stored locally only. HeartHero saves your check-in history in your browser using localStorage. No account required. No data is ever sent to a server, cloud database, or third party.
      🗑️
      You control your data. Clear your entire history at any time using the "Clear All" button. Clearing your browser's local storage also removes all HeartHero data permanently.
      ⚕️
      Not a medical tool. HeartHero is a personal tracking tool, not a medical diagnosis. It cannot replace a doctor's evaluation. If you experience severe symptoms or a medical emergency, call emergency services immediately.

      ⚕️ Medical Disclaimer

      HeartHero is a personal tracking tool, not a medical diagnosis. It cannot replace a doctor's evaluation. If you are experiencing severe chest pain, trouble breathing, fainting, or a medical emergency — call emergency services immediately.
